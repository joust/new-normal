Installation

New Normal is a so-called PWA, a progressive web app. This means that you can install the game as an app on your mobile phone or tablet - without an App Store - using the following instructions for your mobile operating system.

iOS

  1. Launch Safari on your ‌iPhone‌ or ‌iPad‌.
  2. Open the adress new-normal.app.
  3. Tap the Share icon (the square with an arrow pointing out of it) at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Scroll down to the list of actions and tap Add to Home Screen.

Android

  1. Launch the “Chrome” browser app.
  2. Open the adress new-normal.app.
  3. Tap the menu icon (3 dots in upper right-hand corner) and tap Add to homescreen.
  4. You'll be able to enter a name for the shortcut and then Chrome will add it to your home screen.
